Privacy Policy

Dosekin — Family Pill Reminder
Last updated: 11 July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Dosekin ("the App", "we", "us"), published by Hivecrawl Studio, collects, uses, stores, and protects your information. By installing or using the App you agree to the practices described below.

Health data notice. Dosekin is designed to manage medicine schedules and health conditions for you and your family. Information you enter is sensitive. We treat it as confidential, do not sell it, and do not use it for advertising.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information you provide

  • Family & profile data — names, nicknames, or icons you create for family members.
  • Medicine data — medicine names, dosages, schedules, reminder times, and intake logs (taken / skipped / missed).
  • Health condition reports — symptoms, notes, and doctor-report entries you record.
  • Photos — pictures of medicines you capture with the camera or select from your gallery.
  • Voice input — audio you record for voice notes and the transcribed text derived from it.

1.2 Account information

  • When you sign in for cloud backup, we use Google Sign-In and receive your Google account email address and a unique user identifier via Firebase Authentication.

1.3 Information collected automatically

  • Device / push token — a Firebase Cloud Messaging token used to deliver reminders and notifications.
  • Technical data — network connectivity state and basic device information needed for reliable syncing and notification delivery.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Deliver medicine reminders and notifications on schedule.
  • Store and display your medicine schedules, logs, and health reports.
  • Generate doctor reports and summaries from data you entered.
  • Back up and sync your data across your devices when you sign in.
  • Maintain, secure, and improve the reliability of the App.

We do not sell your personal data, and we do not use it for advertising or profiling.

4. Data Storage & Cloud Services

Your data is stored locally on your device. If you enable backup / sign-in, it is also stored in your private cloud space using Google Firebase (Authentication, Cloud Firestore, Cloud Storage, Cloud Functions, and Cloud Messaging). Data in transit and at rest on Firebase is protected by Google's encryption and security infrastructure.

Firebase processes data under Google's terms. See Firebase Privacy & Security and Google Privacy Policy.

5. Sharing & Disclosure

We do not share your personal or health information with third parties, except:

  • Service providers — Google Firebase, which hosts backup and notification infrastructure on our behalf.
  • Legal requirements — when required by applicable law or valid legal process.
  • With your action — when you export or share a doctor report or backup file yourself.

6. Data Retention

Local data remains on your device until you delete it or uninstall the App. Cloud-backed data remains in your account until you delete it in the App or request account deletion. Uninstalling the App does not automatically delete cloud backups.

7. Your Rights & Choices

  • Access & edit — view and edit your data directly in the App.
  • Delete — remove individual records, or delete your account and associated cloud data.
  • Account deletion request — contact us (Section 10) to request full deletion of your cloud data.
  • Withdraw consent — sign out to stop cloud sync, or revoke device permissions.

8. Children's Privacy

The App may be used by adults to manage medicines for children in their family, under the adult's control. The App is not directed to children as independent users, and we do not knowingly collect data directly from children without parental supervision.

9. Security

We use industry-standard safeguards, including encrypted transmission (HTTPS/TLS) and authenticated access to cloud data. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.
